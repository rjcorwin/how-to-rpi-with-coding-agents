# Mascot Plushie Manufacturers

Research into manufacturers that can turn the pink pixel mascot character into a plush toy.

## Single / Small Batch (1-off personal plushie)

- **Budsies** (https://www.budsies.com/) — Min order: 1. Artwork-to-plush, great for a single personal plushie.
- **Child's Own Studio** (https://www.childsown.com/) — Min order: 1. Handmade artisan plushies from drawings (4-5 week turnaround).
- **MyPlushBuddy** (https://www.myplushbuddy.com/) — Min order: 1. No minimum, handmade from artwork.

## Small-to-Medium Batch (merch / gifts)

- **MakeMyPlush** (https://makemyplush.com/) — Low/no minimum. **Explicitly supports pixel art** — best match for the pixel mascot.
- **Bespoke Plush** (https://bespokeplush.com/) — 1 to 100k. High-detail character plushies from original designs.
- **Stuffed Animal Pros** (https://www.stuffedanimalpros.com/) — 1 to 10k+. Personal project manager, end-to-end support.
- **CustomPlushMaker** (https://customplushmaker.com/) — No minimums, fast shipping.

## Sell-on-Demand

- **Fourthwall** (https://fourthwall.com/design-and-sell/custom-plushies) — No minimum. They handle production, fulfillment & shipping. No inventory needed. Starts at ~$16/unit.

## Recommendation

**MakeMyPlush** is the top pick since they explicitly support pixel art designs. For a single personal plushie, **Budsies** is also a great choice.
